Students attending quizes with their best effort:

Every month Munna's Academy arranges Quiz for all the classes including Bangla and English Version to check the students' accurance and improvement in specific subjects. The students' creativities are also checked along with their Class attendance and Notes which makes the student more attentive to his/her studies. In the Quizes, there is also a grading system known as 'CPI' like the NCTB education system. Video presentation, project making as well as writing report are also included in the Quiz. All these systems helps the student to get the clear idea of their new curriculum assessment.
